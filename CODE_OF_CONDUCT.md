# Code of Conduct

Be respectful, specific, and constructive. Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct directed at others, doxxing, and publication of private information are not accepted in project spaces.

Discuss code and product decisions on their merits. Assume good faith, make room for correction, and avoid sharing illegal, graphic, or sensitive model output in issues or pull requests. Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and restrict participation when needed to protect the community.

Report conduct concerns through the repository's private maintainer contact or GitHub reporting tools after publication.
